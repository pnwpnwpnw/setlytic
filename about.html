About us

We've done the manual version. We'd rather build the automatic one.

Setlytics started after one too many bookings managed through a chat thread, a spreadsheet, and a prayer. We build the systems we wished existed back then.

Our story

Built from real small-business chaos, not a textbook

Before Setlytics, we were the ones building one-off booking systems for local shops, workshops, and clinics — using nothing but Google Forms, Sheets, and whatever messaging app the owner already lived in.

We noticed the same pattern everywhere: great businesses, drowning in admin that had nothing to do with why customers loved them. So we turned that pattern into a repeatable way of working — and Setlytics became the team that does it properly, every time.

What we believe

The principles behind every system we build.

01

Use what you already have

We almost never ask a client to switch tools. The best automation fits the workflow you're used to.

02

You stay in control

Automation should remove busywork, not your judgment. Every system leaves a clear point where you decide.

03

Simple beats clever

If your team can't understand it in five minutes, we haven't finished the job yet.

04

Speed matters

Most projects go live within two weeks. Admin pain doesn't wait for a long roadmap, so neither do we.
