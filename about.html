About us

We saw that beauty chains had no real CRM. So we built one — inside LINE.

Setlytics is a CRM agency for multi-branch beauty and nail brands. We build the customer management system that the industry never had — one that runs inside LINE, scales across branches, and doesn't cost a fortune.

Our story

The CRM gap nobody fixed — until now

Beauty and nail brands with multiple locations have a problem no one talks about: they have no real CRM. Customer history is buried in LINE chats per branch. Staff can't see whether a customer is a first-timer or a VIP with 20 visits. Loyalty is tracked in a notebook, if at all.

Enterprise CRM software is built for retail chains and hotels — not for a nail brand with three branches in Bangkok. We built Setlytics specifically for this: a CRM that lives inside LINE Mini App, scales across every branch, and costs a fraction of what enterprise software charges. Because your customers deserve to be remembered by name.

What we believe

The principles behind every system we build.

01

LINE is the platform

Your customers already live in LINE. The best CRM is the one they'll actually open — we build it there, not in a separate app they'll never download.

02

Every customer deserves to be known

It shouldn't matter which branch they walk into. Their history, preferences, and loyalty follow them — that's what a real CRM does.

03

Simple enough for any staff to run

If the person at the counter needs training to look up a customer, the system failed. Ours should feel obvious in thirty seconds.

04

Built for your brand, not for everyone

We don't sell a generic SaaS you configure yourself. We build the CRM around your branch structure, service menu, and loyalty rules — then hand it over.
